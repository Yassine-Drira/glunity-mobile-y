# Future Roadmap: High-Value Features (Can Be Added Later)

**Document Version**: 1.0  
**Target Audience**: Product Managers, Executive Stakeholders & Client Roadmap Planning  
**Objective**: Comprehensive catalog of 12 advanced features missing from both Glunity Mobile and Glutenia, designed for future platform updates (Phase 2 & Phase 3 Roadmap).

---

## 1. Multi-Allergen Custom Filtering (Beyond Gluten)
* **Description**: Custom profile filters for secondary intolerances (Dairy/Lactose, Soy, Nuts, Eggs, Sesame, Corn).
* **Value**: Celiac patients frequently suffer from secondary food intolerances. Scanning and recipe filters will flag multi-allergen risks automatically.

---

## 2. Offline-First Barcode & Ingredient Scanning
* **Description**: On-device SQLite/WatermelonDB caching for top 50,000 product barcodes and on-device OCR model execution.
* **Value**: Allows users in grocery store basements and low-connectivity areas to scan product safety without active cellular data.

---

## 3. Medical Report Export for Doctors (PDF / CSV)
* **Description**: Automated generator producing an official **Gastroenterologist Symptom & Reaction Log**.
* **Value**: Patients can export PDF reports showing exposure dates, symptom severity, and diet compliance to share during medical consultations.

---

## 4. Production Payment Gateway Integration (Stripe / Flouci / Konnect)
* **Description**: Integration with PCI-compliant payment providers (Stripe, Konnect, Flouci) for credit cards and mobile wallets.
* **Value**: Enables real-world financial transactions, automated seller payouts, and platform commission processing for the seller marketplace.

---

## 5. Crowdsourced Barcode & Product Submissions
* **Description**: Community submission tool allowing users to snap photos of unlisted products and submit them for admin verification.
* **Value**: Drives exponential product database growth via user contributions while maintaining admin quality control.

---

## 6. Emergency Contamination & Product Recall Push Alerts
* **Description**: Real-time broadcast system sending urgent push notifications when a manufacturer issues a gluten cross-contamination recall.
* **Value**: Protects user health and positions the platform as the authoritative safety authority for the celiac community.

---

## 7. Granular Kitchen Safety Verification (Cross-Contamination Checks)
* **Description**: Verified restaurant safety badges detailing specific kitchen protocols ("Dedicated Gluten-Free Fryer", "Separate Prep Area", "Certified Staff Training").
* **Value**: Eliminates fear of cross-contamination when dining out at non-100% gluten-free establishments.

---

## 8. GPS Geofenced Proximity Alerts
* **Description**: Background location listener notifying users when they walk within 200 meters of a 100% certified gluten-free bakery or restaurant.
* **Value**: Boosts foot traffic for partner establishments and improves user discovery while traveling.

---

## 9. Multi-Profile Caregiver Household Management
* **Description**: Single account structure allowing parents or caregivers to manage sub-profiles for children with individual symptom logs and diet rules.
* **Value**: Simplifies household management for families with multiple celiac members.

---

## 10. "Buy Recipe Ingredients" One-Click Cart Import
* **Description**: Action button on recipe detail screens that automatically adds all required gluten-free ingredients to the shopping cart.
* **Value**: Bridges content discovery directly with seller marketplace revenue.

---

## 11. Live Delivery Order Tracking
* **Description**: Real-time GPS map tracking and status updates for orders placed from local gluten-free bakeries and stores.
* **Value**: Modernizes the buyer delivery experience to match modern e-commerce standards.

---

## 12. Conversational AI Dietitian & E-Number Checker
* **Description**: Interactive AI chatbot providing instant answers to additive safety (e.g. "Is E1422 safe?") and meal ideas based on available pantry items.
* **Value**: Keeps users engaged inside the app as a 24/7 personal celiac consultant.
